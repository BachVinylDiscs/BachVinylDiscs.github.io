The Jungle Book (1967)


More info about the movie

Why is this my favorite movie?

It's my favorite because I watched it often as a toddler. We have it in videocassette form at home, so there are good memories of putting into the VCR.
I like the cartoon-ish form, the background music, and the songs sung by the monkeys.

Favorite Singer

My favorite actor (well, singer here) is Phil Harris, who sings "Bare Necessities" by Baloo, the bear. It's a catchy song, and it captures his supposed opinion on how to live.
